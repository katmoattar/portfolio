About Me

I'm Katherine Moattar, a Digital Marketing Associate II and Digital Product Owner in Las Vegas, NV. My work sits at the intersection of marketing strategy, product thinking, and data — where I design and run programs that reduce friction and drive measurable growth.

I started as a digital acquisitions intern and have grown into owning end-to-end A/B and multivariate experimentation roadmaps, leading cross-functional product launches, and developing omni-channel marketing strategy across email, push, in-app, and landing pages for credit card and insurance products.

I graduated Cum Laude in 2022 with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, specializing in Marketing, from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. During my time at UNLV, I was a member of the Honors College and an active leader in the UNLV American Marketing Association — where I collaborated with a team to develop real-world marketing strategies for actual companies, helping bring UNLV to the finals of the AMA International Collegiate Competition.

I'm currently open to product marketing, growth marketing, and lifecycle strategy roles where experimentation and customer empathy are at the core.
